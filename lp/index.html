Six systems. One healthspan engine.

The Curriculum

18 live sessions. 4 phases. One protocol.

A structured 18-week program that takes you from the science of longevity to a personal protocol you can measure. Taught live, in small classes of 8 to 15. All sessions are recorded.

Phase 1 · Sessions 1–3

Foundation

Aging as a modifiable trajectory. Lifespan versus healthspan. Baseline your energy, mobility, clarity and resilience with three field-ready self-assessments.

Phase 2 · Sessions 4–7

Data & Discovery

Glucose variability and metabolic flexibility. Apply a continuous glucose monitor. Distinguish patterns from noise across 14 days of your own data.

Phase 3 · Sessions 8–13

Lifestyle Systems

Time-restricted eating, the microbiome, movement, sleep, stress, cardiovascular risk. The six systems, applied to your life.

Phase 4 · Sessions 14–18

Integration & Blueprint

Read a longevity-grade lab panel. Build your personal protocol. Translate the blueprint into a 90-day execution plan with peer accountability.
